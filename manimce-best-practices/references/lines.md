# Lines and Strokes

## Line Guidance

- Use lines and arrows to explain relationships, not decoration.
- Keep stroke widths visible after compression.
- Use line style consistently within a scene.

## Good Uses

- pointing to a target
- connecting related objects
- indicating direction or flow
- highlighting a boundary or axis

## Pitfalls

- tiny arrows that disappear in compression
- too many connector lines
- mismatched stroke weights across related elements
